Soy honey chicken thighs

Description

A very simple yet delicious recipe that will melt in your mouth.
It will take you no more than 20 minutes to prepare for two.

Ingredients

  • chicken
  • soy sauce
  • honey
  • garlic
  • brocoli

Steps

  1. Season the chicken with salt and pepper (let chicken rest for 12-24 hours).
  2. Heat up pan and pour oil of your choice.
  3. Cook chicken.
  4. Chop garlic and brocoli.
  5. Remove chicken from pan once its cooked and cook brocoli on the same pan.
  6. Remove brocoli and but a little more oil on the pan to cook the garlic.
  7. Add a quarter cup of soy sauce and a little less that a quarter cup of honey.
  8. Add chicken back in the pan and let it reduce.
  9. Enjoy!
