Roasted potatoes

Description

Are your potatoes always boring? Do you wish your potatoes came out crispy on the outside but soft in the middle?
Well, worry no more with this roasted potato recipe!

Ingredients

  • potatoes
  • salt
  • water
  • ghee
  • parsley
  • chives

Steps

  1. Peel potatoes and put them in a pot with water.
  2. Put a good amount of salt on the pot.
  3. Bring to a boil for 5-15 minutes.
  4. Drain your potatoes and try to cool and dry them.
  5. On a cast iron put some ghee.
  6. Sear the potatoes on the cast iron.
  7. Season with salt and black pepper.
  8. Roast them in the oven for 20 minutes
  9. Drain potatoes to get rid of excess fat.
  10. Garnish with parsley and chives.
  11. Enjoy!
